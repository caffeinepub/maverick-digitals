# Design Brief

## Direction
Maverick Digitals Premium Agency — immersive dark digital experience with neon-infused glassmorphism, handcrafted asymmetric layouts, and cinematic micro-interactions.

## Tone
Luxury tech maximalism executed with restraint — bold neon accents on ultra-dark surfaces, editorial composition, premium without excess.

## Differentiation
Glassmorphic depth layering with deliberate neon edge highlights and scroll-linked parallax creates cinematic storytelling that feels handcrafted, never templated.

## Typography
- Display: Space Grotesk — bold hero headlines, section titles, premium tech aesthetic
- Body: Inter — clean content, readable at all sizes, modern sans-serif
- Mono: JetBrains Mono — technical accents, code blocks, subtle details
- Scale: hero 56–64px, h2 32–40px, label 12–14px, body 14–16px

## Elevation & Depth
Glassmorphic cards (0.13 L, 12–16px backdrop blur) with 1–2px white/neon borders, layered shadows (0 0 24px neon glow), and edge highlights; every section has deliberate surface treatment — no flat defaults.

## Structural Zones

| Zone    | Background                    | Border                | Notes                                      |
| ------- | ----------------------------- | --------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| Header  | 0.09 L with subtle gradient   | bottom 1px border     | Sticky glassmorphic nav with CTA           |
| Content | Alternating 0.09 L / 0.12 L   | —                     | Section depth variation for rhythm        |
| Cards   | 0.13 L with 12px blur + glow  | 1px white/neon accent | 3D tilt, parallax on scroll                |
| Footer  | 0.12 L with top border        | top 1px line          | Multi-column layout, subtle hover effects  |

## Spacing & Rhythm
6–8px grid with 24–48px section gaps; content grouped in 16–32px clusters for visual hierarchy; negative space used intentionally to prevent visual overload on dark surfaces.

## Component Patterns
- Buttons: rounded-lg, gradient fill (purple→cyan), glow on hover, smooth 0.3s transition
- Cards: rounded-lg, glassmorphic base, neon border glow, shadow-elevated, 3D-tilt on hover
- Badges: inline neon accent text, no background, subtle letter-spacing

## Motion
- Entrance: fade + scale-in on scroll reveal, 0.6s ease-out
- Hover: scale 1.02, glow intensify, border brighten, 0.3s smooth
- Decorative: subtle parallax (2–5px offset per section), floating micro-animations on hero

## Constraints
- Glassmorphism only on interactive/content cards; never on typography
- Neon glows must be restrained (4–24px blur) to avoid washing out content
- No bounce/elastic animations; all motion is smooth and intentional
- Dark surfaces require 7:1+ contrast for AA compliance; neon text only for interactive elements

## Signature Detail
Neon-bordered glassmorphic panels with edge-only glow create dimensional depth on ultra-dark backgrounds — premium luxury tech aesthetic that rejects flat modernism.
